Visitor Information & Zones

منطقه فرهنگی تیاکاهو

Zone A: Tiacauh Cultural Area

Tiacauh Tribal Reserve: Please respect the rich local tribal culture. It is currently the season of traditional "physical dialogue" (duels). Approaching or attempting contact with the tribes for tourism purposes is extremely dangerous.

سایت میراث تاریخی

Zone B: Historical Heritage Site

Historical Relic Reserve: Preservation area for the colossal machine of the past, "Big Ugly". Unauthorized removal of parts or attempts at modification will be severely punished under Sargon Cultural Protection Laws.

منطقه حفاظت شده اکولوژیکی

Zone C: Ecological Preservation

Ecological Preservation Area: Habitat for rare Originium-mutated flora. Due to high concentrations of active Originium radiation in the vicinity, all civilian nature tours are currently suspended indefinitely.
